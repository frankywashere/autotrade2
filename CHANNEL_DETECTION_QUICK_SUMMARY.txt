QUESTION 2: What operations does channel detection involve?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ANSWER: For EACH of 8 windows, detect_channel() performs:

1. Linear regression on OHLC (scipy.stats.linregress) - MOST EXPENSIVE
2. Residual calculation: close - center_line
3. Standard deviation: std(residuals)
4. Upper/Lower bounds: center ± 2σ
5. Bounce detection (detect_bounces):
   - Loop through all bars
   - Check HIGH vs upper boundary
   - Check LOW vs lower boundary
   - Count alternations, cycles, touches
6. Quality metrics: alternation_ratio, slope_pct, width_pct

PER-WINDOW COST: O(n) linear regression + O(n) bounce detection = O(n)
TOTAL FOR 8 WINDOWS: 8 × O(n) = expensive!
